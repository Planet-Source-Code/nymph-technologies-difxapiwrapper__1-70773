Steps to get the Test Project working.

1. Rename the difxapi_dll to difxapi.dll found in DIFxAPI Dll folder.

2. Copy the difxapi.dll to your System32 folder. (usually "c:\windows\system32")

3. Copy the Test Project to your "c:\" drive and test.

** NOTES **

** 1 ** 

you can omit step 1. if you downloded the DriverInstallationTools and installed to default location "c:\program files\Driver Installation Tools 2.01" then you must copy the difxapi.dll from  "c:\program files\Driver Installation Tools 2.01\DIFxAPI\x86" folder to your System32 folder.

** 2 **  

difxapi.dll is also part of Windows DDK.
